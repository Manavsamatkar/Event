Short Detail About Institude.

Swaminarayan Siddhanta Institute of Technology is established with an aim to provide engineering education with a difference. It was founded in the year 2008 by the Sarvasiddhanta Education Society aspiring of disseminating knowledge in the fields of Engineering and Technology in Nagpur. The college is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education and is affiliated to RTM Nagpur University. SSIT offers six Under-Graduate Programmes in the fields of Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Computer Engineering and Information Technology and one Post-Graduate Programme viz. Master of Computer Application.

                                                                                              

Short Detail About Teachers Day.

Teachers are the medium of knowledge. The responsibility of spreading literacy amongst children and adults lies on their shoulders. One might think that reading on various topics online is sufficient to learn about a subject, but that is not true at all. Without teachers, there will be no foundation for our education. To mark the importance of teachers, Teachers’ Day is celebrated annually on the 5th of September in India. It is the birthday of Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan – the second president of India. Students celebrate Teachers’ Day with much vigour and enthusiasm. Skits and musical performances are held in schools to commemorate the occasion. Students also present their teachers with gifts and cards. Speeches are given on the significance of teachers. Former students call on their teachers to show their respect and love for them. Teachers are also awarded prizes on this day.
